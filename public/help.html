Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! 100% free. We're currently in beta, and all users who sign up now get grandfathered status - meaning you'll have free access to ALL features forever, even after we launch paid tiers.

Do I need to create an account?

No! You can create and download signatures without an account. However, creating a free account lets you save multiple signatures, auto-fill your info, and access them from any device.

My signature looks different after pasting. Why?

Some email clients modify HTML slightly. Make sure you're using the "Copy HTML" button (not downloading). If issues persist, try the "Email to Me" option which includes specific instructions for your email provider.

Can I use my company logo?

Yes! Just paste the URL of your logo image (must be hosted online). We recommend using a logo that's at least 200px wide for best quality.

Can I edit my signature later?

Yes! If you create an account, all your signatures are saved. Just click "My Signatures" and click "Edit" on any signature to make changes.

Will this work on mobile?

Yes! Your signature is mobile-responsive and will look great on all devices. However, you'll need to install it from a desktop/laptop computer first.
